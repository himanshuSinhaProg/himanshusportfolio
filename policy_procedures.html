MHS Inc. Workplace Bullying and Harassment Policy Statement 1. Purpose and Scope MHS Inc. is dedicated to fostering a safe, respectful, and supportive work environment for all employees. We believe that a workplace free from bullying, harassment, and intimidation not only enhances morale but also contributes to overall organizational effectiveness. This policy serves to establish clear behavioral standards and demonstrates our commitment to prevent, address, and resolve any instances of bullying and harassment. Our policy ensures all employees, whether permanent, temporary, casual, contract, or student workers, understand the expectations regarding acceptable behavior and the steps they should follow if they experience or witness unacceptable behavior. This policy applies to all work-related activities, including but not limited to in-person interactions, telecommunication, digital or electronic communication, and company-sponsored events, whether they occur on or off the premises. 2. Policy Statement MHS Inc. maintains a zero-tolerance stance against any behavior that could be deemed bullying or harassment. We are committed to ensuring all employees work in an environment of respect, trust, and inclusivity. Our organization does not tolerate any behavior or communication, either intentional or unintentional, that could harm, intimidate, or demean individuals within our workforce. Any reported incident of harassment or bullying will be investigated impartially and addressed in a timely manner, adhering to principles of fairness and confidentiality. 3. Definition of Bullying and Harassment To prevent misunderstandings, MHS Inc. provides the following definitions and examples. Bullying and harassment are defined as any acts, behaviors, or communications that a reasonable individual would recognize as likely to cause humiliation, intimidation, or distress to another person in the workplace. These behaviors may be direct or indirect and could involve individuals at any level within the organization.
