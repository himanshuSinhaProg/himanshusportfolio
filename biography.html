Himanshu | Data Analytics Consultant & Strategic Decision-Maker Himanshu helps businesses make better decisions: a data wizard, a consultant, and a natural marketer. Himanshu employs numerous quantitative techniques using numerical and analytical methods to arrive at actionable insights from heaps and heaps of data to steer executives, organizations, and businesses in the right direction. Proficient in Data Visualization tools like Power BI, Tableau, and Excel. Himanshu has created interactive dashboards and compelling storytelling narratives that complement the decision-making ability of the end-users. Professional Experience & Technical Expertise Born in New Delhi, Himanshu completed his Electronics and Communication Engineering Bachelor before working at Ericsson as a Network Data Analyst. There he worked on cutting-edge telecommunication technologies like 5G and LTE, analyzing Network KPI data and diagnosing and predicting faulty nodes using Machine Learning and AI. Optimizing Network traffic and nodes to maintain the speed and reliability of the Telecommunication Network was one of the many responsibilities Himanshu undertook during his time at Ericsson. He completed his Post Degree Program in Artificial Intelligence from Durham College, Toronto before transferring to Camosun College in Victoria to finish his management education, while working as a Process Consultant for a Toronto-based Psychometric Assessments Publisher.
